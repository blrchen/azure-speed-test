Privacy policy

Azure Speed Test respects your privacy. This page outlines the limited information we collect when you visit azurespeed.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Privacy policy details

We believe in transparency and simplicity. Our approach to data collection is minimal; we only gather what's necessary to provide you with the best experience while respecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide personal details. When you load the site, we gather non-identifying analytics such as the pages visited, approximate location derived from IP address, browser type, and device characteristics.

When you run a latency test, network requests are issued directly from your browser to Azure regional endpoints; the results stay in your session and are not persisted on our servers.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage solely to remember your measurement preferences (selected regions, units, and filters). These values remain in your browser and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted infrastructure providers to serve static assets and collect anonymous usage analytics. These partners process data on our behalf under agreements that safeguard your privacy.

We do not sell or share your information for advertising purposes.

Your Choices

You have control over your data

  • Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Block analytics scripts using browser extensions
  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Clear your browser data to remove stored preferences

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on GitHub or email the maintainer at admin@azurespeed.com .
