Frequently asked questions

Who can use Azure US Government regions?

Azure US Government is available to:

  • US federal, state, local, and tribal government agencies
  • Solution providers building solutions for government
  • Companies holding specific government data subject to regulations
  • Qualifying private enterprises with data subject to government regulations
What compliance standards does Azure US Government meet?

Azure US Government meets various compliance standards including:

  • FedRAMP High
  • DOD Impact Level 5 (IL5)
  • CJIS
  • ITAR
  • IRS 1075

For the latest compliance information, visit the Azure Government compliance documentation .

How do I request access?

Start the onboarding process by submitting the Azure Government request form and completing the required validation steps with Microsoft.
